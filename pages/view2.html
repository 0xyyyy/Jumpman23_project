Consumer Behavior

The following is an analysis of consumer behavior in the NYC market.

High Volume Hours

In order to understand what peak engagement hours are for our app I created a timeline of time by order volume.


Takeaway:

Clearly, order volume is spiking once at the lunch hour and again at around 6pm, with consistent orders between these times and steep dropoffs before lunch and after dinner. This further reinforces the idea that restaurants should prep food ahead of time during peak hours, leading to reduced total order time.

Popular Restaurants


Is anyone surprised that shake shack enjoys the highest order volume? I'm not. Using knowledge of consumer preferences can help us create targeted ad campaigns that could help capture new customers for the growing platform in NYC.
